2005. The world needed a trustworthy plant shop; succulent farms were failing left anf right and every flower company was in the red. “the floral industry is over” they said. It was out of this pile of ashes that The Flower Shoppe planted its own roots. We took the idea of a florist back to its core, and invested time and energy into perfecting the craft of growing and distibuting quality plants. We focused on the traditional experience of simply walking into your local plant shop and picking out your next houseplant, and brought it to the real world. The online world. From there, the rest is history. We have been providing that same quality service for over 18 years now and we hope that you will join us in our journey: to deliver the ultimate plant shopping experience.
